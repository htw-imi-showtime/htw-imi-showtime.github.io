Gefahr erkannt, Gefahr gebannt is a VR learning application for teaching occupational safety measures. We developed a virtual reality application which aims to teach safety precautions for trainees in metal workshops. In our simulation, users can process basic materials with various tools modeled after real examples, and must be careful to not hurt themselves. While doing so, they must choose the proper safety equipment to wear while operating their machines as well as operate the machines properly to avoid ‘brutal’ injuries. Can you complete the work assignment while keeping all your fingers?
